About

Hello! My name is Jeanlucas and I am a Computer Science student at the Federal University of Ouro Preto (UFOP). From an early age, I have been fascinated by the potential of technology to transform lives and solve complex problems. Currently, I am focused on web development and software development, areas in which I continuously strive to improve. In addition to my passion for technology, I am an enthusiast of new cultures and languages. I believe that experiencing different cultures enriches our perspectives and makes us more empathetic and understanding. I love learning new languages, as it not only enhances my communication skills but also brings me closer to unique people and experiences. My personal philosophy is to seek authenticity in a world often dominated by artificiality. I strive to maintain a healthy balance, enjoying the benefits of technology without losing sight of the importance of genuine human interactions and authentic experiences.
